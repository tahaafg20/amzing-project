Smoothie Bar

Introduced by Amazin’ Graze, Glow is a convenient tropical smoothie bar offering smoothies, smoothie bowls and customisable yoghurt tubs to all busy urbanites on the go. Designed to create a tropical oasis in the concrete jungle of Kuala Lumpur, Glow aims to inspire our community to feel empowered in their best self, and to glow from within with confidence and health.

Why Us?

Real ingredients

Glow Smoothies are made with real and natural ingredients only. Fibrous fruits and vegetables, protein packed nuts and superfoods together with nut milk, all blended into a nutritious and delightful smoothie which is vegan friendly too.

No refined sugar

The delightful taste of Glow creations comes from nature’s best gift: fruits and natural sweetener such as coconut sugar and dates. Which means it’s lower in GI (Glycemic Index), sustainably sourced, and non artificial.

Whole fruits

Fruits are the star of all Glow smoothies. Using whole fruits as our base, each cup of Glow smoothie comes without ice creams, sorbets, or flavoured yoghurt. We try our best to source them locally as much as we could, for sustainability and ultimate freshness.
