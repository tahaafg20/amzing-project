Our Story

How it all started

This is the story of three young women that came together because of their belief that food should be life-giving and empowering. After working in many demanding corporate roles in Asia, Amy, Ching and Sabrina became increasingly frustrated at the unhealthy lifestyles and food options around them. So one day, the three girls decided to take things into their own hands. After leaving their corporate jobs, they decided to create healthier food alternatives for this growing group of urban, ambitious & conscious community. They began in their home kitchen and created a classic range of granolas and nut mixes that are still available today. Today, a small dedicated team of food lovers work together to produce fresh, delicious foods and constantly innovate to take everyday food to a new level using only the best, most nutritious ingredients. The result? Food that nourishes, empowers, and gives consumers the confidence to discover themselves and the vibrant world around them.

Traditional Rolled Oats

Oats function as the 'base' of each Amazin' Granola, giving it hearty flavour, texture, and valuable nutrition. Whole rolled oats, which are used in every Amazin' Granola, are packed with soluble fiber, to keep cholesterol levels healthy and give you a slow but steady source of energy to last the day.
