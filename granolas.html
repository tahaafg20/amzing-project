Low Sugar Wild Strawberry Granola

Low Sugar diet has been on everyone’s mind, and we want to introduce a Low Sugar range made with only natural sweeteners that are actually good for you, without any nasties you can’t pronounce or odd taste that will affect your food, because healthy can be tasty too. So we have Monk Fruit as our natural sweetener, which has zero calories and doesn’t spike up your blood sugar levels. These granola clusters are infused with fresh strawberries and raspberries, which has less than 5% sugar for those who are looking for a less sweet breakfast or snack option. Mixed in with strawberries, white melon seeds and unsweetened coconut chips for flavours and boost of nutrient.

Coconut Kaya Granola

Discover Delight: sit back and feel the delight as you crunch into our Coconut Kaya Granola. Experience silky-smooth golden Coconut Kaya caramelized over wholesome blend of rolled oats, buttery pecans, and earthy almonds and smell the sweet Asian aroma of pandan.

Matcha Green Tea Granola

A special collaboration with our friends Nikoneko from Malaysia who bring in these premium pure matcha green tea powder exclusively from Nagoya. This beautiful East-Asian inspired granola is full of the goodness of green tea, chia seeds, dried cranberries and nourishing sweet longan.
